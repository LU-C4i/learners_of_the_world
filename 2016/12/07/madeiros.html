Rethinking International Tax System should be mandatory for every person dealing with taxes, ie, to virtually everyone. This trailblazer course debunks some of the key concepts regarding tax law and foster tax stakeholders to think fast and slow about the challenges posed by the 4thRevolution and how the frictions between different tax jurisdictions and lack of harmonization generate deeply and negative externalities among European taxpayers as whole.

It is a fact that nowadays people don’t tolerate any more multinationals that use legal schemes and loopholes to avoid paying their “fair share” to the community. Name and shame is no good to any company and can cause irreparable damages. This course sheds priceless guidance about this very hot topic and thanks to Professor Douma and his Leiden colleagues I came up with the idea of creating a Tax Code of Conduct for my company, which was very well welcomed by the C-room.
